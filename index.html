LA UNION

La Union (Tagalog pronunciation: [la ˈuɲon])
Officially the Province of La Union (Ilocano: Probinsia ti La Union; Pangasinan: Luyag na La Union; Kankanaey: Probinsyan di La Union; Ibaloi: Probinsya ne La Union; Tagalog: Lalawigan ng La Union), is a province in the Philippines located in the Ilocos Region in the Island of Luzon. Its capital is the City of San Fernando, which also serves as the regional center of the Ilocos Region.

FAQ

Q. I am overseas Filipino visitor. Do I need visa?

A. Overseas filipino and foreigners who are spouses or children of overseas Vietnamese or Vietnamese citizens are exempt from visa requirements to enter Vietnam. Visitors in this group are allowed to stay visa-free for up to 6 months.

Q. How do I apply for an electronic visa?

A. 4x6cm digital front facing, white background photo without glasses
     Scan of passport details page (passport for at least 6 months more)

Q. Is La Union a good destination for solo female travelers?

A. La Union is one of the safest destinations for solo female travellers in the region. La Union people are for the majority, non-confrontational, and are quite shy around foreigners.

Q. What are the most common sickness travelers get in La Union?

A. Heat-related illnesses such as heatstroke and dehydration can be an issue in the hot season. Stay vigilant about hydration and applying sunblock.

Q. How much should a taxi from the city cost?

A. A round-trip taxi from San Fernando City’ should cost between 300 to 400 pesos and last about from 30 to 50 minutes.

Q. How long does it take to travel from Manila to La Union and vice versa by vehicle?

A. If you were to stay onboard the whole journey, it would technically take you 7 to 8 hours

Q. When is the best time to visit La Union?

A. The months of April and May offer sunshine and a refreshing breeze, while September to October are favoured for their cooler temperatures and clear skies.

Q. What is the best thing to do in La Union besides trekking?

A. An exhilarating way to experience the La Union is via mountain bikes, doing sightseeing, or you can do baths from beaches and rivers.
