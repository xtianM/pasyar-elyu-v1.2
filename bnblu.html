Bahay na Bato The house that was constructed along the coast of La Union is called “Bahay na Bato”, it is entirely made from pebbles and stones that were found along the sidewalks and the beaches of the city’s municipality. The city is called Pebble Capital of the North because stone-picking is its most famous and booming industry. Aside from the painted pebbles, the wooden sculptures and the stone foundation that was made by Bong Kim, a Korean, it also has interiors and an underground that is the secret hideaway of the Noble’s.
